By setting your Scopus identifier (Author ID) in Scientilla, the system is going to find automatically the documents linked to your Scopus profile, including metadata (citations, ...). Documents imported from Scopus will be shown in the External section. Both the documents imported from Scopus and documents already present in Scientilla that likely belong to you or your research line will be shown in the Suggested section.

To see how to retrieve your personal Scopus identifier, please watch the video and fill the Scopus identifier field. Click here to look for your identifier.
