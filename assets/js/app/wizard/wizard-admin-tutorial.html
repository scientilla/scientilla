If you are a Principal Investigator or a Facility Coordinator, you are going to certify also the scientific production of your group (research line or facility), in addition to your personal profile.

To switch between the personal and the group profile, just click on the arrow next to your profile's name and select the desired profile, as shown in the image.

In the Suggested section, you will find automatically the publications already added by your team members to their personal profiles: you can verify them directly, adding them to the scientific production of the group.

You can also check and modify your team members.
