Scientilla is our platform to access and manage both career and research information.

Scientilla is designed for both research and administrative staff.

In Scientilla, research and administrative staff can keep track of their previous and current career steps and/or check and certify their scientific production. Research staff can keep the scientific production up-to-date independently and have a complete overview of the past and present activities. Group leaders can find the data of the team easily accessible and can better design their strategy.

Ultimately, information about scientific output including publications, fund raising, technology transfer and HR data will be available in Scientilla.

Scientilla ensures the data driving our strategic decisions are trusted, comprehensive and accessible in real-time. By collecting in a single place varied information, it offers a wide and detailed view of the activities of groups, research lines and all staff.

Please use Scientilla with the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Opera, Safari or Edge.
